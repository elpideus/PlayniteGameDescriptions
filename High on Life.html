From the mind of Justin Roiland, co-creator of Rick and Morty, comes High on Life – a wild sci-fi shooter that combines comedy and chaos. Step into a universe where talking guns, wacky characters, and bizarre missions are the norm. The alien cartel is after humanity, and it's up to you to defend Earth and take down the G3, an extraterrestrial threat that won’t back down easily.

Features

  • Dynamic Combat: Engage in frenetic battles with alien foes, wielding unique talking guns that evolve and offer comedic commentary.
  • Expansive Worlds: Traverse a variety of alien landscapes, each brimming with peculiar life forms, quirky characters, and intricate lore.
  • Immersive Storytelling: Experience humor-infused storytelling with a rich narrative, voiced by some of the most iconic comedians.

With a style reminiscent of animated adult comedies, High on Life is an unpredictable journey of hilarity, alien tech, and action-packed adventure.

What’s New

Since its launch, the game has introduced several updates to enhance gameplay, including new missions and improved performance, ensuring an even more immersive experience.

Whether you’re a fan of sci-fi or comedy, High on Life offers a unique and memorable gaming experience. Get ready to save humanity, one talking gun at a time!
