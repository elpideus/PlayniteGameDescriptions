Honkai Impact 3rd: Embark on an Action-Packed Anime Adventure

Honkai Impact 3rd is a visually stunning action RPG available on Mobile and PC, set in a world on the brink of destruction. Dive into high-speed combat, strategic team formation, and a gripping storyline that blends science fiction with mythological elements. Play as powerful Valkyries and fight against the mysterious forces of the Honkai to protect humanity.

Epic Combat and Stunning Visuals

Engage in fast-paced, combo-driven combat that allows you to switch between characters mid-battle, each with their unique abilities and weapons. With breathtaking graphics, detailed character models, and immersive special effects, every battle feels cinematic. Face off against massive bosses, waves of enemies, and use your Valkyries' skills to create devastating combos that leave a lasting impact.

Rich Story and Engaging Characters

Meet a diverse cast of Valkyries, each with their own backstory, personalities, and powerful abilities. As you progress through the emotional storyline, unlock new characters, and explore their complex relationships with each other. From the determined Kiana to the mysterious Mei, every character brings something unique to the table, making for an unforgettable narrative experience.

Challenging Multiplayer and Co-op Missions

Join forces with friends or other players in real-time multiplayer co-op missions. Tackle challenging levels, fight against world bosses, and prove your teamwork skills as you coordinate with others to overcome tough obstacles. The cooperative gameplay adds depth and replayability to the game, as you strategize and work together to complete objectives and gain rewards.
